About DrupalCampCS

Two days of Drupal action!

What is DrupalCamp CS?
DrupalCamp CS is event organised by Drupal communitites in Slovakia and Czech Republic for fans of Drupal and other web technologies. It's aim is to bring together communities, exchange information, build new relationships by sharing and learning from like minded people in a relaxed environment.

Why do you organise it together?
We are a community of individuals and believe that by collaborating we enhance the community by creating a much bigger and better conference for all.

For who is this event?
DrupalCamp CS is event for everyone with interest in Drupal. For individuals it is a great place to learn, for companies you have the chance to network and explore new business opportunities - no previous knowledge of Drupal is needed we welcome everyone who want to make our community better, we look forward to welcoming you.

Who are organizers?
DrupalCamp CS is bring to you by Slovak Drupal Association and Slovak and Czech drupal communities. We are strongly cooperating with How-Know - project of students organisations Ynet and IAESTE Slovakia. We want to say thank you to Comenius University for providing venue.

Where? When?
DrupalCamp CS is moving event. This year is again being held in Bratislava, Slovakia on Faculty of Mathematics, Physics and Informatics, Comenius University. It will take a place on 28. - 29. May 2016.

Why are we going to Bratislava again?
We decided to hold even again in Bratislava, because Czech part of our team is fully engaged with IronCamp organisation. For 2017 we promise to go to Czech Republic. We heard rumors that there is good beer too :)
